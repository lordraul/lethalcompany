Entities

This page lists entities in Lethal Company.

The majority of the entities are dangerous, however some pose little to no threat to employees if encountered and/or handled correctly. Some can be killed by melee weapons and the Double-barrel shotgun, and some can be stunned by stun grenades or the zap gun.

Entities are divided into three categories.

  • Indoor Entities: Exclusively occur on indoor maps such as The Facility and The Mansion (e.g. Thumpers, Coil-heads, etc.).
  • Outdoor Entities: Encountered outside, usually only at later stages of the day or during eclipses (e.g. Eyeless Dogs, Forest Giants, etc.).
  • Daytime Entities: Can be seen outside all day, no matter the time of the day, (e.g. Manticoils, Circuit Bees, etc.) though some moons cannot spawn them. (e.g. Rend, Dine, Titan)

Entity Properties

  • Health: How much health an entity has. For reference a shovel does 1 damage to entities. Immune means that the entity is invulnerable, or the entity can't be hit (e.g. Ghost Girl).
    • Note: Entity HP and employee HP is not the same stat, as employee damage is calculated separately.
  • Power Level: Power level of the entity, find out more about power levels on Moons
  • Max Spawned: Limits the amount of entities of this type that can exist on the map at the same time.
  • Shock Response: If the entity can be stunned by the Zap gun, Stun grenade or DIY-Flashbang
  • Stun Multiplier: When this entity is stunned by a Stun grenade or DIY-Flashbang, the stun duration is multiplied by this value. (Higher value = longer stun; Lower value = shorter stun; Immune = cannot be stunned)
  • Door Open Speed: The speed an entity opens a closed door is multiplied by this value. (Higher value = faster door open speed; Lower value = slower door open speed) Incapable means the entity is not able to open doors by themselves (e.g. Hygrodere).
  • Favorite Moon: The moon this entity is most likely to spawn on.
