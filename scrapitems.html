Scrap

Scrap is the main collectable found in the facilities. Loot tables define where and how often certain scrap can spawn. Unlike Equipment, Scrap is lost upon team death, this includes items that have a use like Stop Signs and the Double Barrel.


Scrap Properties

  • Weight: The carrying weight of this item. Stamina depletes faster and regenerates more slowly the larger the sum of the weights of the carried items
  • Min / Max Value: The selling price of the scrap when sold to the company at 100% value.
    • Note: The value of items can be heavily inflated if the item is retrieved from a Gift Box.
  • Conductive: Determines if the scrap can be struck by lightning in Stormy weather
  • Two handed: Determines if the scrap will give you the Hands full status.
  • Battery: Determines if the scrap has a battery that can deplete over time or by being used.
  • Info: Small summary or important facts about the scrap.
